"The Catastrophe Containment of Lake Sorrow"



When reports emerged of a monstrous aquatic menace terrorizing the shores of Lake Sorrow,
Buri Buri Zaemon responded without hesitation — or prior confirmation.

Arriving on the scene in full ceremonial armor,
he announced the commencement of “Operation Aquatic Justice” to no one in particular.

One rowboat overturned (strategically).

A wave dramatically displaced.

The “sea monster” identified as an unusually shaped log.

Undeterred by factual clarification,
Buri Buri Zaemon proclaimed the lake officially liberated.

The waters were calm shortly afterward.

Historians continue to debate whether the calm was natural
or a direct consequence of his overwhelming heroic presence.
